Training Interface

Overview

In addition to the task interface, you will also have a training interface on the right side of the screen that you can use to receive task instruction from the teacher.

Allowed Actions

Within this interface, you can take three kinds of actions to get instruction from the teacher: take action in the task interface, request a demonstration, and verify that the task is complete .

Taking an action in the task interface

You can take an action directly in the task interface and the teacher will provide you with correctness feedback. If they approve of the action you took, then you will be allowed to continue. However, if they disapprove of the action, then you will have to try a different action.

When you are waiting for feedback from the teacher, the task interface will lock (denoted by a lock symbol in the bottom right of the task interface). When the teacher is finished giving you feedback the interface will unlock again.

Requesting a demonstration

If you're not sure how to proceed, then you can press the "request a demonstration" button in the training actions menu in the bottom right. This will ask the teacher to provide you with an example of a correct next action. When the teacher is generating the demonstration the task interface will lock and the teacher's demonstration will be shown directly in the task interface. After the teacher has given you a demonstration the interface will unlock and you will be allowed to proceed.

Verify task is complete

When you think you have completed the task, press the "verify task is done button" in the training actions menu in the bottom right. If the task has been completed and the teacher approves of this action, then you will be allowed to proceed to the next action. However, if the task is not done and the teacher disapproves, then you will have to continue working on the task.
