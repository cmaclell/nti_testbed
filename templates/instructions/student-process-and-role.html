Student Role and Process

Role Overview

Congratulations, you have been randomly assigned to the role of student for this experiment! Your job will be to learn from an AI system how to perform a novel task. Before describing the task and training interface, this page is meant to give you a brief overview of the structure of the experiment.

Experimental Process

See the following figure for a depection of the four phases of the experiment.

You are currently in the instruction phase. Once you finish, the AI system will teach you to perform a task in the context of three scenarios. After you complete this training, then you will be tested on your knowledge in three novel scenarios. Your performance on these test scenarios will determine your bonus, so make sure to do your best to learn the task correctly and demonstrate your knowledge in these test scenarios. Finally, you will complete a questionaire about your experience in this experiment.
