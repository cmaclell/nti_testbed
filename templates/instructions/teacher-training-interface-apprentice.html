Training Interface

Overview

In addition to the task interface, you will also have a training interface on the right side of the screen, shown in the images below.

Within this interface, you will provide students with three kinds of instruction: demonstrations, action approval, and task completion approval.

Demonstrations

The image below shows an example of a student requesting a demonstration. When this happens, you can take an action in the interface to show them the correct next step.

Action Approval

The image below shows an example of a student requesting approval for an action they have taken. When this happens you click one of the buttons in the bottom right to either approve or disapprove of their action. When you approve, the student will be allowed to continue with the task. However, when you disapprove they will have to try a diferent action before they can proceed.

Task Completion Approval

The image below shows an example of a student requestion approval for task completion. If the student has correctly completed the task, then approving them will allow them to move on to the next task scenario. However, if you disapprove then they will have to continue working on the task before they can proceed.
