Teacher Overview

Congratulations, you have been randomly assigned to the role of teacher for this experiment! Your job will be to teach an AI system to perform a particular task, which will be described to you shortly.

Before describing the task and training interface, this page is meant to give you a brief overview of the structure of the experiment. See the following figure for a depection of the three phases of the experiment.

The instructions phase is where you currently are. Once you finish the instructions, you will then teach the AI student to perform the same task in the context of three scenarios. Finally, you will be asked to complete a questionaire regarding your experience teaching.

After you complete the experiment, we will test the AI student that you taught to see if it can complete the same task in three novel scenarios. The performance of the AI student on these test problems will determine your bonus, so make sure you do your best to teach it well!
