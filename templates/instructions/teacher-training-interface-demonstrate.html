Training Interface

Overview

In addition to the task interface, you will also have a training interface on the right side of the screen, shown here.

Within this interface, you will provide students with demonstrations of how to perform the task.

Demonstrations

To instruct the AI student, provide them with examples of how to correctly perform the task directly in the task interface

Additionally, you can press the "Mark Task Complete" action in the bottom right menu to specify that the task has is finished. When you mark a task complete, it will move the student on to the next scenario.

Interaction Log

Throughout the training process all interactions with the AI student will be recorded in the training interaction log shown in the images above.
