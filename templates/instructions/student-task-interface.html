Task Interface

Overview

For this experiment, you will be trying to learn to perform a particular task given the AI system's guidance, so these instructions will not describe the task to you. However, these instructions are just meant to give you a brief introduction to the interface you will be using to perform the target task.

Allowed Actions

When you go through the training, you will learn more about what you are supposed to do in this interface, but for now you just need to know that the following actions are allowed.

  • Clicking on the upper part of the screen
  • Pressing the buttons on the bottom when they are active
